Our Journey

Futbol Club Barcelona Femení is the women's football section of FC Barcelona, competing in Liga F. Founded in 1970, the club evolved from local roots into one of the most dominant forces in European women’s football.

Officially incorporated into FC Barcelona in 2002, the team has grown rapidly — claiming national and continental glory, inspiring new generations, and showcasing the excellence of women’s football.

Home matches are played at the Johan Cruyff Stadium, and the team proudly continues the club’s legacy of equality, strength, and sporting greatness.
